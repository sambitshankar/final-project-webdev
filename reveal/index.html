Life is too short. We spend all our time living someone else's life. We try to act, walk and talk like them. We try to put up fake smiles and we tend to overlook the beauty inside us. Remember, life is too short to worry about a failed test or an embarrasing fall before a few people the other night. What life is though, it is long enough to spread a smile across someone's face, it is long enough to make things right and its long enough to realize your own worth and be happy. Having said that, take up the quiz and reveal who you truly are. Who knows, this will make you discover something about yourself that you have never realized! Have fun and remember - always keep smiling!
